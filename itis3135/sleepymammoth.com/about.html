About Us

Sleepy Mammoth is a luxury loungewear brand dedicated to reviving the ancient elegance of mammoth fur—ethically sourced via time-traveling ecologists who regularly visit and study the ice age—transforming it into sumptuous sleepwear that blends primal luxury with modern comfort. Each piece in our collection is painstakingly crafted: the fur’s natural insulation keeps users cool in summer nights and cozy in winter, while its weightless softness evokes the raw beauty of a mammoth’s coat. Thoughtfully designed cuts, muted earthy tones, and minimalist detailing make Sleepy Mammoth’s garments both timeless and effortlessly stylish.

Rooted in both respect for nature’s history and an eye toward sustainable luxury, Sleepy Mammoth works with paleo-conservation scientists and preservationists to ensure that all materials are responsibly sourced and processed. The brand’s mission is not only to offer wearers a transcendent sleep experience—slipping into a cocoon of prehistoric warmth—but also to spark wonder for our planet’s deep past, marrying craftsmanship, comfort, and conservation in every stitch.
